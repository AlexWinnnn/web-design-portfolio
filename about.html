Brief Background

I am a junior at the University of Denver enrolled in the MFJS 3501: Web Design & Content Development course. I am learning the ins and outs of website creation through building website skeletons and turning them into accessible web pages.

This course is my first experience working with code, specifically HTML, but I am excited to see the progression. I am a Journalism major who loves to the travel the world and hopes to write seven books, one on each continent.

Creating this website is helping me better understand and appreciate the interconnectedness that website are, and this site is a basic example of my beginnings in that.

My Goals for This Course

  • Learn how to adjust basic HTML from the same code into new pages and meanings
  • Setup and understand the navigation between pages to create an interconnected site
  • Develop a unique, accessible, and workable website
